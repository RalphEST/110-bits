About this resource

With the transition with online learning, we noticed it might be nice to have some additional asynchronous material for you (our students) to utilize as you learn about probability. Please find below a library of video material that you can use as a supplement to your studying for this class. Godspeed.

To navigate this page, simply choose and click on the week you're looking for in the table of contents! The videos are organized on a per week basis. Each video will have some description associated with it, including the topics it covers and its type. Some videos are 'reviewing content' videos that go over the concepts you've seen in lecture and in the textbook, with a few examples to support the theory. Other videos aim to explain the solution to a textbook, homework, section, or strategic practice problem.
